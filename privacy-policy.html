01Who we are

FX Globe ("we", "us", "our") operates the FX Globe mobile application (the "App"), an informational and educational platform connecting trading signal providers with consumers. FX Globe does not execute trades, hold user funds, or provide regulated financial advice.

Contact us anytime at fxglobe@cimkoconsulting.com.

02Data we collect
Account info
Name, email, phone, date of birth, profile picture
Authentication
Login method (email/password or Google Sign-In), session tokens
Provider verification
KYC submission data, verification status, country
App activity
Signals posted or viewed, follows, saved signals, notification interactions
Device data
Push notification token, device type, app version
Payment data
Once VIP subscriptions launch: transaction records via our payment processor — we do not store full card details
03How we use it
  • To create and manage your account, and to distinguish Consumer, Provider, and Admin roles
  • To deliver signal content, follower notifications, and platform updates via push notification
  • To calculate provider performance metrics (accuracy, win rate) shown on profiles and the leaderboard
  • To verify provider identity through our KYC process before granting provider status
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or violations of our terms, and to enforce account bans where necessary
  • To communicate with you about your account, support requests, or policy changes

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

05Retention & your rights

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account — in-app or via this site — your data is permanently and immediately removed, including your login itself. There is no waiting period. The only exception would be specific records we're required to retain by law (for example, once paid subscriptions launch, certain financial records may be subject to statutory retention periods).

  • Access, correct, or update your account information at any time within the app
  • Request deletion of your account and data from within the app (Settings → Privacy & Security → Delete Account), or via our Account Deletion page
  • Opt out of push notifications via your device settings
06Children's privacy

FX Globe is a financial information platform intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

07Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or via email.
