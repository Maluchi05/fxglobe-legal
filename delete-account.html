Account & Data Deletion

Delete your account and data

You can request permanent deletion of your FX Globe account and all associated data at any time. This page explains what gets deleted, how long it takes, and how to submit your request.

Email us to request deletion

Opens your email app addressed to our support team, pre-filled with the details we need.

How to request deletion

  • Tap the button above, or email fxglobe@cimkoconsulting.com directly from the address linked to your FX Globe account.
  • Include the email address you signed up with, and whether you have a Consumer or Provider account.
  • We verify the request came from the account owner, then permanently delete the account and its data.
  • You'll receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete, usually within 7 business days.

What gets deleted

Consumer accounts Profile, saved signals, follows, notification tokens, interaction history
Provider accounts Profile, posted signals, follower relationships, analytics, KYC records

Before you request deletion

  • If you have an active paid subscription, cancel it first — deleting your account does not automatically stop billing. Mention any active subscription in your request and we'll help you cancel it as part of the process.
  • Deletion is permanent. Signals you've posted, your follower history, and your performance stats cannot be recovered once removed.
  • Some information may be retained where we're legally required to (for example, transaction records for financial compliance), even after the rest of your data is deleted.
